About

The third and most epic edition of ECHOES is here! A full week packed with everything you can think of — dance, music, drama, sports, art, literary battles, informal games, Mr. & Ms. Echoes, and the much awaited star nights. From high-energy competitions to crazy fun nights, there's something for everyone.

So mark your calendars 13th to 16th December — one of India's biggest medical fests is calling. Are you ready to feel the fever?
